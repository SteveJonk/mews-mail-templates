Did you forget your favorite stuffed animal or came home with only 1 shoe on your foot? We work together with Faundit to reunite you and your item. Click on this link and leave your details.

Faundit is on a mission to bring back favourite things. Besides the parcels that they send, Faundit is a sustainable service. With every lost package that they send back, Faundit plants a tree in return!
