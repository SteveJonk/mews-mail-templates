YOU’RE CHECKED-IN

To make your stay memorable, we want to share some extra information about Hostel Roots & Tilburg. You can book these extras to make your stay complete during the online process!

Is there anything not right? Anything we missed? Any questions or you just want to have a talk? We are here for you! Find us at the reception. We are always happy to help

Our bar is hidden just underneath street level. Here, you can still find the “old roots” of the building. Before we opened as a hostel, the building functioned as a bank. The old vault is still here so come take a look!

Sunday – Wednesday 07.30PM – 09.00PM
Thursday – Saturday 07.30PM – 11.00PM

We serve cold drinks, wines and of course some nice beers. La Trappe Beer is not just a beer. It is made in a Trappist Brewery! The beer is specially made by monks. In Berkel-Enschot, just a 20 minute bike ride away!

Another very typical Tilburg drink is Schrobbelèr. A liquor made from 43 different herbs and spices! Officially made in the 70’s as a medicine. Now we drink it with every special occasion! ;)
